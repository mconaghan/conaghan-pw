Welcome to My Paths!

My Paths allows you to record your paths. Whether it's a walk, run, ski or cycle the MyPaths Android app will record where you go and let you take photos, videos and record annotations. You can then view (and share if you want) your paths online.

The site is still in development, contact mconaghan@gmail.com if you would like to know when the site is ready for beta testers
